ABOUT US

London Restaurant was started in 1966 by founder Johnny Yamaguchi. Since his founding, Johnny has provided high-quality cuisine from multiple countries with the aim of being enjoyed by many people regardless of nationality. Today, head chefs from around the world offer high-quality courses, and the elegant taste and beautiful presentation of carefully selected ingredients have been highly praised by customers. In addition, at our restaurant, you can choose from a wide variety of sake selected by a first-class sommelier, and you can enjoy wine, gin, and sake for each course. The calm atmosphere of the store is perfect for spending special time with important customers, and we promise to provide a wonderful and memorable time. We are looking forward to your visit.
